Coles Insurance Service Centre

About

Coles Insurance Service Centre is a secure and easy to use customer platform allowing customers to login using a one time pin to their email address or mobile phone number.

Using a human centred design approach to ensure that this product was right for the customer, Coles Insurance customers are now able to make changes to their insurance, download documentation and maintain their policies online hassle free with a simple login process.

After only just one week of being live, CISC had over 1000+ customer registrations with over 500+ policy changes and continues to grow.
