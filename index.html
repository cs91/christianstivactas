About me

After graduating from the University of Wollongong and completing my Information Technology graduate position I knew I wanted to create amazing experiences for customers using cutting edge technology.

I decided to become a Digital Business Analyst & Iteration Manager at Insurance Australia Group one of the largest general insurance companies in Australia. Some would call me a jack of all trades Scrum Master (SCM) with an understanding of the end-to-end customer journey and how to make it come to life with the use of technology.

Coles Insurance Service Centre

A secure and easy to use customer platform allowing customers to login using a one time pin to their email or mobile phone number.

Using a human centred design process to ensure that this product was right for the customer, they are now able to make changes to their insurance, download documentation and maintain their policies online hassle free.

WILMA - Wesfarmers Insurance Motor Allocations

Wilma is a motor lodgement and repairer allocation application used by over 200+ consultants everyday to lodge insurance claims for clients.

It faced numerous challenges integrating multiple processes into one easy to use application. Consultants were always the forefront of building this application to ensure they were able to perform their roles quickly and with little frustration.

Coles Insurance - Emergency Online Claims

Emergency online claims provides customers with an easy hassle free process to lodge a claim online in the event of a major event or catastrophe saving time to the customer and giving them immediate peace of mind.

The application was built with both the IAG supply chain in mind creating a repairer allocation immediatley for the customer.

Coles Insurance - Express Quote

Customers wanted a way to get the quickest quote online so we built it for them. Express quote provides and quick and easy insurance quote process using third party APIs to gain instant information for the customer.

Using the newest technologies we were able to provide one of the fastest insurance quotes in the market.
